About

I am a PhD student in the School of Computer Science at Georgia Tech advised by Dr. Ahmed Saeed. My research spans large-scale systems and datacenter resource management, with current work focusing on managing slack resources for datacenter efficiency. My goal is to increase datacenter resource harvesting opportunities and improve energy efficiency while keeping SLOs intact.
